No-Churn Chocolate Ice Cream

Indulge in the creamiest, most decadent No-Churn Chocolate Ice Cream you can make at home! This rich dessert is made without an ice cream maker, and it's so easy to prepare. No need to worry about churning or waiting hours for it to freeze – just mix the ingredients, pour into a pan, and freeze. In just a few hours, you’ll have a rich, creamy chocolate ice cream that’s perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

1 can sweetened condensed milk

1 cup heavy cream

1/2 cup cocoa powder

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Pinch of salt

Instructions

1. In a large bowl, whisk the heavy cream until it forms stiff peaks.

2. In another bowl, combine the sweetened condensed milk, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Stir until smooth.

3. Gently fold the whipped cream into the chocolate mixture until well combined.

4. Transfer the mixture to a loaf pan or container and smooth the top.

5. Cover and freeze for at least 4 hours or overnight until firm.

6. Once frozen, scoop and enjoy your delicious No-Churn Chocolate Ice Cream!

It’s that simple! You’ll have a homemade ice cream treat that is smooth, creamy, and incredibly chocolatey. It’s a perfect dessert for hot summer days or whenever you crave a sweet indulgence!
